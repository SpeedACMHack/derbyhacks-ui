ATTENDEES

We welcome everyone at DerbyHacks no matter your skill levels. We will have mentors walking around to help participants that are having trouble and plenty of workshops to help you get started! This event is free and open to students of all majors. Come meet new friends, learn, and create awesome things.

RULES

We require that all entries be created from scratch, during the competition. The use of 3rd party libraries and APIs is allowed and encouraged. Teams are limited to four people. To provide hackers with the best possible environment for learning, networking, and creating we adhere to the MLH Code of Conduct.
